Frequently Asked Questions About Holistic Orthodontic

How much do holistic orthodontic treatments at Nikaeen Orthodontics typically cost, and do you offer payment plans?
The cost for holistic orthodontic treatment at our Los Angeles office varies depending on the complexity and duration of your care. On average, comprehensive treatments such as Invisalign or braces generally range from $4,500 to $8,000. We understand that affordability is important, so we offer flexible, interest-free payment plans and work with most PPO dental insurances. During your initial consultation, we’ll provide a personalized price estimate and discuss all available payment options.
What is the typical timeline for holistic orthodontic treatment at your Los Angeles office?
Most patients at Nikaeen Orthodontics can expect their holistic orthodontic treatment to last between 12 and 24 months, depending on their specific needs and the complexity of their case. During your first visit, Dr. Nikaeen will perform a thorough assessment using digital imaging and discuss your customized treatment plan, including estimated timelines. We prioritize a gentle, whole-body approach, so treatment may include additional steps such as airway assessments, myofunctional therapy, or nutritional guidance, which can influence the overall duration.
Is Dr. Nikaeen licensed and experienced in providing holistic orthodontic care in California?
Yes, Dr. Atoosa Nikaeen is a board-certified orthodontist licensed to practice in California. She holds a Doctorate in Dental Surgery (DDS) and completed her orthodontic residency at UCLA. With over 20 years of experience, Dr. Nikaeen is dedicated to holistic, patient-centered orthodontic care, integrating advanced techniques and whole-health principles. Our practice is fully compliant with all California dental and orthodontic board requirements.
What is the process for starting holistic orthodontic treatment at Nikaeen Orthodontics?
To begin, you’ll schedule an initial consultation at our West Los Angeles office. During this visit, Dr. Nikaeen will review your medical and dental history, perform a comprehensive exam, take digital x-rays, and discuss your health goals. We focus on underlying causes of misalignment, including airway health and facial development. Based on your assessment, we’ll design a personalized treatment plan that may include braces, aligners, or adjunctive therapies. You’ll receive a detailed explanation of each step before any treatment begins.
