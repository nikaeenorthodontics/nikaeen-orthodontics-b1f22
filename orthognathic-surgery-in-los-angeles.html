Frequently Asked Questions About Orthognathic Surgery

How much does orthognathic (jaw) surgery typically cost at Nikaeen Orthodontics in Los Angeles?
The cost of orthognathic surgery at Nikaeen Orthodontics can vary significantly depending on the complexity of your case and whether both jaws are involved. On average, the surgical portion can range from $20,000 to $40,000 in Los Angeles. This does not include orthodontic treatment (braces or Invisalign), which often adds $5,000 to $8,000. We work with several reputable oral surgeons and can help coordinate estimates and insurance pre-authorizations. Some insurance plans may cover a portion if the surgery is medically necessary, and our team will help you navigate the coverage and financing options.
How long does the entire orthognathic surgery process take from consultation to full recovery?
The full process for orthognathic surgery at Nikaeen Orthodontics typically takes 18 to 24 months. It starts with an initial consultation, followed by a pre-surgical orthodontic phase (usually 9–12 months). The surgery itself is performed by a partnered oral surgeon, after which you'll need about 6–8 weeks for initial healing. Post-surgical orthodontic treatment lasts an additional 6–12 months to fine-tune your bite. Our team will closely guide you through each phase and coordinate with your surgical team to ensure a smooth, well-planned treatment.
Are the orthodontists and surgeons at Nikaeen Orthodontics licensed and experienced with orthognathic surgery cases?
Absolutely. Dr. Atoosa Nikaeen is a board-certified orthodontist with over 20 years of experience, including extensive work on complex orthognathic cases in Los Angeles. We collaborate with board-certified oral and maxillofacial surgeons who are licensed in California and specialize in corrective jaw surgery. Our team regularly works with local surgical centers and hospitals, ensuring your care is in the hands of professionals with proven expertise.
What is the process if I want to start orthognathic surgery treatment at your Los Angeles office?
The process begins with a detailed consultation at our West Los Angeles office. We’ll take digital x-rays, 3D scans, and photographs to assess your bite and jaw alignment. If orthognathic surgery is recommended, we’ll create a coordinated treatment plan with you and your chosen oral surgeon. Pre-surgical orthodontics (braces or Invisalign) will align your teeth for surgery. After surgery, you’ll return to our office for follow-up orthodontic treatment and monitoring. Throughout, we coordinate all appointments and communication with your surgeon to ensure seamless care.
Is there a guarantee on the results of my orthognathic surgery and orthodontic treatment?
While every case is unique and surgical results can vary, Nikaeen Orthodontics stands by our commitment to achieving the best possible functional and aesthetic outcome for you. We provide detailed pre-treatment planning, digital simulations, and post-surgical reviews to ensure you understand expected results. Minor adjustments are often included in your orthodontic treatment fee for up to one year after appliance removal. However, as with any surgery, factors like healing and individual biology can affect long-term results, so we encourage ongoing follow-up visits for optimal stability.
