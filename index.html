Frequently Asked Questions About Braces

How much do braces typically cost at Nikaeen Orthodontics in Los Angeles, and are payment plans available?

During your complimentary consultation, we’ll provide a detailed cost breakdown and discuss all available financing options.
How long will I need to wear braces if I start treatment at your Los Angeles office?
The average treatment time for braces at our Los Angeles office is between 18 and 24 months. However, the exact duration varies based on the complexity of your case and your specific orthodontic goals. Dr. Nikaeen will discuss a personalized timeline with you during your initial consultation, and we’ll provide regular updates on your progress throughout your treatment.
Is Dr. Nikaeen a board-certified orthodontist, and what qualifications does your team have?
Yes, Dr. Atoosa Nikaeen is a board-certified orthodontist with advanced specialty training and over 20 years of experience practicing in Los Angeles. You can feel confident you’re receiving expert care from a licensed, well-qualified team.
What is the process for getting braces at Nikaeen Orthodontics?
Your journey begins with a complimentary consultation at our Los Angeles office, where we take digital X-rays, photos, and 3D scans to assess your needs. Dr. Nikaeen will discuss your treatment options and create a custom plan. Once you decide to move forward, we schedule an appointment to place your braces, which usually takes about 1-2 hours. You’ll then visit us every 4-8 weeks for adjustments and progress checks. Our team provides guidance on care and answers questions every step of the way.
Do you offer any guarantees on the results of braces treatment, and what happens if I’m not satisfied?
We are committed to achieving the best possible outcome for every patient. While individual results can vary, we stand by our work and will address any concerns you have during or after your treatment.
