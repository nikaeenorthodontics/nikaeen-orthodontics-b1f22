Frequently Asked Questions About Retainers

How much do retainers cost at Nikaeen Orthodontics in Los Angeles, and does insurance cover them?
At Nikaeen Orthodontics, the cost of retainers typically ranges from $250 to $450 per appliance, depending on the type (clear plastic, Hawley, or bonded). Some dental insurance plans in Los Angeles may partially cover retainers if they are part of your orthodontic treatment. We recommend contacting your insurance provider for details. Our office staff is happy to help you check your benefits and discuss payment options or financing if needed.
How long does it take to get my retainer after my braces are removed?
Once your braces are removed at our Los Angeles office, we take precise digital scans or impressions of your teeth. Retainers are custom-made and typically ready within 7–10 days. We'll schedule a follow-up appointment for you to try on your new retainer, ensure the fit is perfect, and provide instructions for wear and care.
Is Nikaeen Orthodontics licensed to provide orthodontic and retainer services in California?
Yes, Dr. Atoosa Nikaeen is a board-certified orthodontist licensed by the Dental Board of California. Our practice complies with all state and local regulations for orthodontic care, and our staff regularly completes continuing education to stay current with the latest techniques and standards.
What is the process for getting a replacement retainer if I lose mine in Los Angeles?
If your retainer is lost or damaged, contact our office as soon as possible. We’ll schedule you for a quick appointment to take new digital scans or impressions if needed. Replacement retainers are usually ready within a week. Since quick action is important to prevent teeth shifting, we prioritize these appointments for our Los Angeles patients.
Does Nikaeen Orthodontics guarantee the fit and effectiveness of my retainer?
We stand by the quality and fit of our custom retainers. If your retainer does not fit properly or causes discomfort within the first 30 days, we will adjust or remake it at no additional cost. However, please note that guarantee does not cover loss or accidental damage. Our team will also provide detailed guidance to maximize your retainer’s lifespan and effectiveness.
